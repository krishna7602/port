Dr. Prangya Ranjan Rout served as a Visiting Fellow/Professor at the University of Manitoba, Winnipeg, Canada. He holds B.Tech and M.Tech degrees in Biotechnology and a Ph.D. in Environmental Biotechnology/Engineering. His research interest lies in the domain of bioreactor design, anaerobic digestion, bioconversion of wastes to wealth, emerging contaminants removal, membrane technology, resource recovery and reuse, and wastewater treatment. Till date he has over 100 scholarly contributions, including refereed journal articles (47), granted patent (1), design patent (1), published patent (1), filed patent (1). edited books (2), book chapters (29), national (17) and international conference (12) presentations to his credit.
